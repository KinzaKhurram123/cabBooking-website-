Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a ride?

+

Simply download the RideLynk app, enter your pickup and drop-off locations, choose your preferred cab type, and confirm your booking. A driver will be assigned to you within minutes.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept credit/debit cards, digital wallets, UPI, net banking, and cash payments. All online transactions are secure and encrypted.

Can I schedule a ride in advance?

+

Yes! You can schedule rides up to 7 days in advance. Perfect for airport pickups, important meetings, or early morning commutes.

What if I need to cancel my ride?

+

You can cancel your ride anytime before the driver arrives. Free cancellation within 2 minutes of booking. After that, minimal cancellation charges may apply.

Are your drivers verified?

+

Absolutely! All drivers undergo comprehensive background checks, license verification, and vehicle inspection before joining RideLynk. Safety is our top priority.

Do you offer corporate accounts?

+

Yes, we offer special corporate packages with dedicated account management, monthly billing, and detailed ride reports. Contact our business team for more details.
